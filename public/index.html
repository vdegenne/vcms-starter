Welcome

If you see this page, it means your app is up and running. Good job.

But this page you reading right now is in the public directory of your application.

You can remove or edit this page to your own likings. Or use the script yarn attach-polymer to call a Polymer starter. Polymer lets you make wonderful things on the front-end spotlight. After attaching a polymer starter, refresh this page and you should see a new welcoming message.
